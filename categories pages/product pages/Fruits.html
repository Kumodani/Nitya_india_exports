Dehydrated Fruits

Dehydrated fruits are fruits that have had most of their water content removed through drying methods like sun drying, air drying, or freeze-drying. This process preserves the fruit, extends its shelf life, and concentrates its natural sugars, making it sweeter and more calorie-dense.
