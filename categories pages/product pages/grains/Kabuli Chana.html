Description

Nitya India Exports is a renowned supplier and exporter of high-quality chickpeas, also known as "Kabuli Chana." Our chickpeas are rich in essential nutrients, making them a perfect choice for a healthy diet. We ensure the best quality through meticulous sorting, cleaning, and packaging, catering to global demands with excellence.

    Nutritional Powerhouse

  • Rich in Protein: A great plant-based protein source.
  • High in Fiber: Supports digestion and maintains gut health.
  • Essential Minerals: Contains iron, magnesium, and folate.
  • Low in Fat: Mostly polyunsaturated, promoting heart health.

    • Sourcing and Processing

    We employ advanced processing technology to clean and sort Kabuli Chana to meet the highest standards. Our environmentally friendly practices and efficient waste management systems further enhance product quality.

    Global Reach and Export Destinations

    We export high-quality Kabuli Chana to several countries, including Malaysia, Indonesia, the Philippines, Thailand, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Turkey, Russia, the USA, Canada, and many more.

      Uses and Benefits of Kabuli Chana

  • Supports Heart Health: Helps reduce cholesterol levels.
  • Regulates Blood Sugar: Beneficial for diabetes management.
  • Rich in Antioxidants: Protects against cell damage.
  • Bone Strength: High in calcium and phosphorus.
  • Weight Management: Keeps you fuller for longer.
  • Gluten-Free Alternative: Used in gluten-free recipes.
