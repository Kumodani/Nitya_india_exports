GROUND SPICES

Ground spices are dried spices that have been finely crushed or milled into a powder form. This process enhances their ability to blend easily into dishes and release flavor quickly. Common ground spices include cinnamon, turmeric, cumin, and black pepper. They are widely used in cooking and baking to add aroma, taste, and color.
