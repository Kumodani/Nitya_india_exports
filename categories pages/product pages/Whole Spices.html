WHOLE SPICES

Whole spices are dried seeds, fruits, roots, or bark of plants used in their unground form to flavor food and beverages. Common whole spices include cinnamon sticks, cloves, cardamom pods, peppercorns, and cumin seeds. They are often used in slow-cooked dishes, pickling, or ground fresh for maximum potency.
